# Research 001: Project Motivation & Problem Statement

## Origin
The project started after observing the physical and mental toll on nursing staff through social media documentation (anecdotal evidence of burnout) and analyzing the lack of automated support systems in high-pressure environments.

## who are burnout?
Merriam-Webster says that **burnout** as "exhaustion of physical or emotional strength or motivation usually as a result of prolonged stress or frustration.".
**burnout** is a state of physical, emotional, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged, unmanaged workplace stress, characterized by energy depletion, increased mental distance from work, and reduced professional efficacy.
- Physical Exhaustion: Persistent fatigue, insomnia, headaches, and muscle pain.
- Mental/Emotional Detachment: Feelings of cynicism, negativity, and reduced professional efficacy.
- Behavioral Changes: Increased absenteeism, isolation, decreased productivity, and irritability.

## The Problem: Burnout and Healthcare Crisis
According to [SciELO, Angel of Hippocrates], nursing burnout leads to:
1. Increased medical errors.
2. High staff turnover (nurses leaving the profession).
3. Decreased quality of patient care.

increasing the risk of death and the number of days of hospitalization, in addition to, as mentioned before, the increased workload.

## Proposed AI Solution
My goal is to create a "digital layer" of monitoring that filters noise and provides actionable insights, reducing "alarm fatigue" and allowing human professionals to focus on complex decision-making.
