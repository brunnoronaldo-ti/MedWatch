# research 003: Sectors of a Hospital

## how have here?

This file contains explanations of how these hospital departments differ. (Emergency, ICU, Ward)

### Emergency (Pronto-Socorro / Emergência):

- It is the entry point for acute cases, accidents, or situations of immediate risk. The patient arrives, is assessed (triage), stabilized, and then discharged or referred to another area of ​​the hospital.

### ICU (UTI - Unidade de Terapia Intensiva): 

- Acronym for Intensive Care Unit. Designed for patients in critical condition or at risk of death. It is the sector with the most advanced technology and continuous monitoring (24 hours) by a highly specialized team.

### Ward (Enfermaria / Ala de Internação):

- É a unidade padrão de internação para pacientes que necessitam de cuidados contínuos, mas não estão em estado crítico. É onde ficam as camas hospitalares comuns para recuperação e acompanhamento clínico até a alta médica.

## in summary

Generally, the hospital flow works in this order: the patient arrives at the Emergency Room, is stabilized, and then transferred to the ICU (if critical) or to a ward (for recovery).
