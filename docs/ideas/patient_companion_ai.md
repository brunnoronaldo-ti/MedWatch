# ideas 001: ai assistent (for patients)

## the idea
 
### an AI 

inspired by Chloe from game "detroit: become human" (that android from the menu that looks at the camera and talks to the player. That's basically an interactive digital avatar.). Imagine this in hospitals, a screen near the bedside, the scene would look something like this: "Hi, I'm the hospital assistant. Your vital signs are stable. If you need anything, I can call a nurse."

It's not just a robotic voice lost in the room. It's a human face, gentle expressions, someone "present".

## Why is this idea so powerful?

### Hospitals have two major problems:

1. Patients become anxious and lonely, especially in the early morning hours.
2. Nurses become overwhelmed.

An intelligent avatar could:

### converse with patients

- talk with pacients
- answer simple questions
- calm anxious people
- explain procedures
- call nurses when necessary

It doesn't replace humans, But it *filters simple requests*.

## Technologies that would allow this

### What I envisioned combines several areas.

### Conversational AI

- to converse with the patient.

### Animated digital avatar

- to show expressions.

### Realistic speech synthesis

- to speak naturally.

### Speech recognition

- to listen to the patient.

All of this is part of the area of **​​Human-Computer Interaction**.  
Today, separate parts of this already exist. What I envisioned is bringing it all together into a functional hospital system.
